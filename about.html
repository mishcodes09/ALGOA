Welcome to Algoa Bus Company

Road transportation of people and communities has and always will be an integral part of any country's working and social life. We provide that essential service for the Nelson Mandela Bay area from our four operating depots. The Company plays a significant role in meeting basic needs as well as building the economy of the region.

Our Mission

At Algoa Bus Company, we are committed to delivering safe, reliable, and affordable transportation services that connect communities and fuel the regional economy.

We uphold professional and ethical business principles, ensuring the growth and sustainability of our company while prioritizing the wellbeing of our employees, customers, and stakeholders.
